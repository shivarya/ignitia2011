BASIC ROBOTICS WORKSHOP PROPOSAL

This is a workshop designed for beginners and robotics enthusiasts .Here you would be introduced to the basic fundamentals of robotics and will learn to apply them in real life. Hands-on sessions will help you understand the practical aspects too. The students will learn the basics concepts and will gain the practical experience while working on the robotics kit specially designed and developed by MITBOTS promoted by Technocracy,IIT delhi

To ignite the passion of robotics in young minds of the country we have designed creative content and robotics kits with manuals.The modules have been designed covering all basic concepts till class 12 that are practically implemented in robotics. The course content has been extensively developed by MITBOTS(team of national robotics champions ,IIT delhi) who has years of experience in robotics industrial and education solutions. It is as follows:

What will you learn ?

• Basic robotics components
• Their working principle with practical implementation
• Basics of Robotics
• The mechanism behind the robots

Apart from this students will also learn the following skill sets:

• Create interest in the field of science and technology
• Encourage creative and lateral thinking
• Encourage experimenting with objects and new ideas
• Encourage students to work in team and do multi-tasking

DETAILED COURSE CONTENT:

Robotics is a field where you can apply your knowledge of physics, chemistry, mathematics, electronics, mechanics etc. This field is the one where your knowledge is put to test.

Basics of Mechanics:

• Torque
• Friction
• Gravity
• Speed
• 2/3/4 wheel driving
• Nuts/Bolts
• Screws
• Gears
• Pulley
• Wheels
• Tracks

Basics of Electricity and Electronics:

• Current and Voltage
• Polarity
• Resistors, Capacitors and Insulators.
• Basic Circuit Building
• LEDs
• Next generation light source
• Making of a electromagnet
• Motors
• Hands on generating electricity from waste material.

Hands on Sessions:

The kit is designed in such a manner, that a student can make more than 10 different types of robots with it. In the hands on sessions through the workshop a student will be assembling these following robots.

Introduction to autonomous robotics

Line follower:

Students will assemble their own line followers and realize working principles through practical's on it.

Competitions

Robo Racing

To enhance the team skills and pratical application ,a robot racing competition will be conducted on international grid track(used in USA).Students will do the racing with a self developed wired robot module.

Duration:

We conduct these on 2 days, each day session, so in all 24 hours properly divided into theory and hands and competitions.
