IGNITIA'11 is not merely all fun and frolic, we feel it is our added responsibility to address issues of social importance.

Being a youth fest IGNITIA'11 makes it a point to target the youth with social issues. IGNITIA11 brings forward the A HUMAN FIRST with a target to educate the under privileged children. The project includes collection of money and helping the underprivileged family to educate their children so that they earn themselves a respectable position in the society. The drive will approach the people willing to provide help in this cause , the alumini and the student of the college as well to take a step forward in educating the the underserved people of the society.
